New Family

Cris being the major dog lover she is. This is her and her husband Tony the day they adopted two more dogs. They adopted them from Little Hills Rescue of Kentucky. Both dogs came from a kill shelter. Now they have THREE!!!! Every one is so happy!!!!

Cristene Giordano

I’m relatively sure Cristene and I met in 1994. I remember meeting her very well. We were in the Hamilton Meijer. She had her Meijer uniform on with her hair pulled up into a ponytail to keep it out of her face. She seemed so young. She was. At that time, she was 21 and I was 30. We became instant friends. I was fixing one of her deli scales and showing her how to resolve the issue without me and we were cracking up. I really can’t remember what we were laughing about, I just remember her contagious laugh and how infectious it was. Soon after that it was often that we were out together. Having a couple of drinks, dancing, listening to 80’s music. It didn’t take long before I realized that Cris had a very deep level of love and respect for all animals and cherished her dogs as family. I remember when she moved out of her parents house and into her own, she had a chocolate lab named Chewy. One day soon after she moved in she left him alone and he literally ATE the couch. The entire couch was all over the livingroom. When you ask Cris what she did to the dog she replies "I didn't speak to him for three whole days!!!"
